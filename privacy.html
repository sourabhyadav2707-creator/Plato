Information we collect

Account information

Name and email address when you create an account or sign in using Google.

Usage information

Reading activity within the platform, streak data, and interests selected during onboarding.

Technical information

Device information, browser type, IP address, and analytics data collected through cookies and similar technologies.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Plato platform
  • Personalize daily briefings based on your interests
  • Track and maintain your streaks and reading activity
  • Improve the performance and user experience of the website
  • Send product updates, daily briefing notifications, and important communications

Cookies & analytics

Plato uses cookies and similar technologies to understand how users interact with the platform and to improve performance. Cookies may collect basic usage and device information.

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Data sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your data may be shared only in the following situations:

  • With service providers that help operate the platform, such as authentication or analytics tools
  • When required by law or legal processes
  • To protect the rights, safety, or integrity of the platform

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. However, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy

Plato is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at any time.
